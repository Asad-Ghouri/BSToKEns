Blockchain Center Of Excellence

BST Group and the European Digital University are opening a blockchain center in Dubai, focusing on Blockchain Technology, Decentralized Eco-Systems, and Startup Acceleration. The center’s primary aim is to educate and facilitate collaboration among industry leaders, entrepreneurs, investors, and public/private sector companies. Key focus areas include:

• Exploring, testing, and implementing blockchain
• Conducting faculty and student workshops
• Conducting joint research
• Publishing papers and reports
• Establishing internships or hiring opportunities
